In the customization section, you can set how long each image appears in your reel. The duration can be set between 0.5 seconds and 10 seconds per image. The default is 1 second.

We offer three transition options:
  • None: Direct cut between images (default)

Choose from three aspect ratios based on your platform:
  • 9:16 (Vertical): Perfect for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

  • 1:1 (Square): Great for Instagram posts, Twitter
üí¨ Can I add text to my reel?
Yes! You can add text overlay that will appear on your reel. The text will be displayed at the bottom center of the video with a semi-transparent background for better readability. Maximum length is 100 characters.

üìè What are the file size limits?
  • Images: Maximum 10MB per image
  • Audio: Maximum 50MB per MP3 file
  • Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG for images
üé¨ What video quality will I get?
All reels are rendered in high definition:
  • 9:16: 1080x1920 pixels (Full HD vertical)
  • 16:9: 1920x1080 pixels (Full HD horizontal)

  • Frame rate: 30 FPS
  • Video codec: H.264 (MP4)
  • Audio codec: AAC

Processing time depends on:
  • Number of images (more images = longer processing)
  • Image duration settings
  • Audio length
  • Selected transitions and effects
Typically, a reel with 10 images takes 30-60 seconds to process.

If your reel plays audio but shows no images, check:
  • Make sure you uploaded valid image files (JPG, JPEG, or PNG)
  • Verify that images are not corrupted
  • Try creating a new reel with different images
  • Check that images are under the 10MB size limit

  • Make sure all files are within size limits
  • Check that your reel name contains only letters, numbers, spaces, underscores, and hyphens
  • Verify that FFmpeg is properly installed on the server
  • Try with fewer images to test
